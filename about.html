The company

Built to build things properly.

Sheen Digital Ltd is an independent software studio incorporated in England and Wales in 2026. We design, develop, and ship mobile applications and web-based software products — working across the full product lifecycle from initial concept through to launch and ongoing iteration.

We are based in Leeds, United Kingdom. Our work is defined by attention to detail, clean engineering, and a focus on building products that are straightforward to use and reliable in practice.

We work with both consumers and businesses, producing software that is built to last — not thrown together. Every product we ship is something we are prepared to stand behind.

Our products

Apps we are building.

We are currently developing our first consumer mobile application, Ataraxia — a daily Stoic philosophy app for iOS and Android. The app features curated quotes, daily reflection prompts, streak tracking, and premium themes.

Ataraxia is built with React Native and Expo, targeting both the Apple App Store and Google Play. Further applications and products are in planning under the Sheen Digital portfolio.
